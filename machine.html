What Shoes Are You Wearing?

We have trained an AI model to guess what kind of shoe it is being presented with. The user would put a shoe up to their camera, and the model would put the shoe into one of ten categories:

Categories: Boots, Cleats, Crocs, Dress Shoes, Flats, Heels, Loafers, Running Shoes, Sandals, Slippers


The model is trained from images of 5-8 different shoes for each category, with 4-7 images per shoe. The images were taken from the Designer Shoe Warehouse (DSW) website.

To learn more about the model, go to the Project Statement page.
